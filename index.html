Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AnnitaPay and how it can benefit your business.

How secure is AnnitaPay for my business?
AnnitaPay employs bank-level security measures including end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits. We're compliant with PCI DSS, GDPR, and other global financial standards to ensure your data and transactions are always protected.
Can I integrate AnnitaPay with my existing accounting software?
Yes, AnnitaPay offers seamless integration with popular accounting software including QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, and more. We also provide API access for custom integrations with your existing systems.
What types of payments can I process with AnnitaPay?
AnnitaPay supports a wide range of payment methods including credit and debit cards, ACH transfers, wire transfers, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cryptocurrency. We're constantly adding new payment options to meet your business needs.
Is there a setup fee or long-term contract required?
No, AnnitaPay has no setup fees or long-term contracts. We offer flexible monthly plans that scale with your business, and you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime without penalties.
How quickly can I get set up with AnnitaPay?
Most businesses can get started with AnnitaPay in under 24 hours. Our streamlined onboarding process requires basic business information and verification. For more complex setups or custom integrations, our dedicated team will work closely with you to ensure a smooth transition.
