Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an AnnitaPay account?

Creating an AnnitaPay account is simple. Download our mobile app from the App Store or Google Play, or visit our website. You'll need to provide some basic information and verify your identity to get started. The entire process takes less than 5 minutes.

What currencies does AnnitaPay support?

We support all major African currencies (NGN, GHS, KES, ZAR, etc.) and global currencies (USD, EUR, GBP). You can also hold and exchange popular stablecoins like USDC, USDT, and BUSD, as well as major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum.

How much does it cost to use AnnitaPay?

AnnitaPay offers free account opening with no monthly fees. Domestic transfers are free, while international transfers and currency exchanges have small, transparent fees that are always displayed before you confirm a transaction. Crypto transactions have network fees that vary based on blockchain congestion.

Is my money safe with AnnitaPay?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade security measures including 256-bit encryption, biometric authentication, and AI-powered fraud detection. Fiat currencies are held with licensed partner banks, and digital assets are stored in multi-signature cold wallets with insurance coverage.

How do I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 through in-app chat, email at support@annitapay.com, and phone in supported countries. You can also visit one of our agent locations for in-person assistance.
