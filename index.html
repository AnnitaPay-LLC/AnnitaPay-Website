Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AnnitaPay services.

What is AnnitaPay?

AnnitaPay is a digital financial service designed to revolutionize payments across Africa. We provide secure, accessible, and affordable financial services for businesses and individuals, even in remote communities. Our platform offers digital wallets, payment gateways, cross-border transfers, and other financial tools tailored for the African market.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account with AnnitaPay is easy and free. Download our mobile app from Google Play or the App Store, click on "Sign Up," and provide the required information such as your name, email, phone number, and a secure password. Verify your identity through SMS or email confirmation, and you’re ready to start using AnnitaPay.

How do I add money to my AnnitaPay wallet?

You can add money to your AnnitaPay wallet through various methods, including linking your bank account, using mobile money services, or visiting a partner agent for a cash deposit.

Is AnnitaPay secure?

Yes, AnnitaPay employs advanced security measures such as encryption, two-factor authentication (2FA), and fraud detection systems to ensure the safety of your transactions and personal information.

How can I withdraw money from my AnnitaPay wallet?

You can withdraw money from your AnnitaPay wallet by transferring it to your linked bank account, sending it to a mobile money account, or cashing out at a partner location.
